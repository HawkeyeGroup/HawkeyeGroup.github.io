CVPR 2017 Workshop : Light Fields for Computer Vision


Spinning Parallelogram Operator for Light Field Depth Estimation

Shuo Zhang, Hao Sheng, Zhang Xiong

We propose a spinning parallelogram operator (SPO) to locate lines and calculate their orientations in an Epipolar Plane Image (EPI) for local depth estimation. The SPO has been demonstrated to be insensitive to occlusion, noise, spatialaliasing, limited angular resolution.
