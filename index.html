Introduction

Hawkeye Computer Vision Group

   The Hawkeye Computer Vision Group is the major computer vision research center in Beihang University. We do research on the basics and applications of computer vision. The research topics include scene segmentation, deep extraction and significant detection based on optical field images, object tracking, motion understanding and video analysis of pedestrian and vehicle based on single/multi camera, object re-identification based on deep learning, and also included the satellite remote sensing image processing, three-dimensional modeling technology, urban large data analysis and IoT etc. We have applied in robot manufacturing, large-scale video surveillance system and so on. Our team has made great progress on these topics and has sought to use these technologies in practical applications.

   We are eager to develop intelligent algorithms capable of performing important visual perception tasks, such as target recognition, scene classification, integrated scene understanding, target motion recognition, and structural understanding.
